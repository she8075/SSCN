01 Product Overview
How it works

SSCN is a tokenized reverse convertible note on ETH/USD. You invest $1,000 per token and receive a guaranteed 10% annual coupon regardless of market conditions.

At maturity, your capital repayment depends on whether ETH has fallen below the barrier:

✓ S(T) ≥ Barrier → $1,000 + $100 coupon = $1,100
⚠ S(T) < Barrier → $1,000 × S(T)/S₀ + $100
