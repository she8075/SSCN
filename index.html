00 Issuance Workflow
1
Deploy Contract
Set barrier, coupon, maturity, max supply and subscription period → deploy on-chain.
S₀ is read automatically from Chainlink at deployment.
2
Publish Term Sheet to Investor(s)
Generate QR code → send to investor(s).
They scan, review the term sheet, choose their amount and deposit USDC on-chain.
3
Wait for Subscriptions
Investors subscribe and lock their USDC on-chain.
Monitor the subscriber list — multiple investors can subscribe up to MAX_SUPPLY.
4
Deposit Coupon Guarantee
Deposit 10% × subscribed supply × $1,000 as coupon guarantee.
Simulates the put premium from selling OTM puts.
5
Close Subscription & Issue Tokens
Close subscription period → KYC verify → issueAll() → tokens appear in investor wallets.
6
Settle at Maturity
Call settleAtMaturity() once maturity is reached.
Chainlink reads S(T) → barrier checked → payoff calculated on-chain → investors redeem.
