01 How It Works
⬇
Deposit
Deposit $1,100 USDC per token. $1,000 is your nominal capital, $100 is the coupon pre-funded from the OTM put premium sold at inception.
⏱
Wait for Maturity
The European barrier is observed only once — at maturity. Intraday volatility does not trigger early redemption. The price is read from Chainlink oracle.
💰
Redeem
No breach: S(T) ≥ B → $1,000 + $100 coupon = $1,100

Breach: S(T) < B → $1,000 × S(T)/S₀ + $100 coupon
Payoff Formula
Scenario 1 — No Breach
Payoff = $1,000 + $100 = $1,100
when S(T) ≥ 60% × S₀
Scenario 2 — Breach
Payoff = $1,000 × S(T)/S₀ + $100
when S(T) < 60% × S₀ · coupon always guaranteed
